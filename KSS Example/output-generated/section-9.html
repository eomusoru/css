9 Tooltips

Dropdown's behavior is mainly defined in css, we just toggle a is-visible class on it when clicking on its toggle button. This toggle button could be any html but must have two attributes:

  • The js-dropdown-toggle class,
  • A data-href attribute containing a CSS selector
Title
Here is some tooltip content
I have a tooltip 
<span data-tooltip="true" class="demo__tooltip">
    <div class="tooltip">
        <div class="tooltip__heading">Title</div>
        <div class="tooltip__content">Here is some tooltip content</div>
    </div>
    <a href="#">I have a tooltip</a>
</span>
